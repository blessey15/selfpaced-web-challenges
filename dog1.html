GERMAN SHEPARD

The German Shepherd is a breed of medium to large-sized working dog that originated in Germany. In the English language, the breed's officially recognized name is German Shepherd Dog. Life expectancy: 9 – 13 years Origin: Germany Colors: Black, Sable, Black & Tan, Red & Black, Grey, Black & Silver Temperament: Obedient, Intelligent, Curious, Loyal, Alert, Confident, Watchful, Courageous Height: Male: 60–65 cm, Female: 55–60 cm Weight: Male: 30–40 kg, Female: 22–32 kg
