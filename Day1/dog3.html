ROTTWEILER

The Rottweiler is a breed of domestic dog, regarded as medium-to-large or large.
The dogs were known in German as Rottweiler Metzgerhund, meaning Rottweil butchers' dogs.
Life expectancy: 8 – 10 years
Origin: Germany
Temperament: Good-natured, Obedient, Devoted, Steady, Fearless, Alert, Confident, Self-assured, Calm, Courageous
Height: Female: 56–63 cm, Male: 61–69 cm
Colors: Black, Tan, Mahogany
Weight: Female: 35–48 kg, Male: 50–60 kg
