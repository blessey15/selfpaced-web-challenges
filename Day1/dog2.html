LABRADOR

The Labrador Retriever, Labrador, or Lab is a medium-large breed of retriever-gun dog.
The Labrador is the most popular breed of dog in many countries around the world.
Life expectancy: 10 – 14 years
Origin: Newfoundland
Colors: Black, Chocolate, Yellow
Weight: Male: 29–36 kg, Female: 25–32 kg
Height: Male: 57–62 cm, Female: 55–60 cm
Temperament: Intelligent, Kind, Agile, Outgoing, Trusting, Even Tempered, Gentle
